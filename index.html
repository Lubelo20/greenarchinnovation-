Our Roots

GreenArch Innovation is an agricultural-tech initiative focused on aquaponics: a sustainable fusion of aquaculture and hydroponics. We use smart farming techniques to grow fresh produce while raising fish with minimal waste.

What is Aquaponics?

Fish produce waste
Bacteria convert waste into nutrients
Plants absorb nutrients
Clean water returns to the fish

It's a closed-loop system smart, clean, and eco-friendly.
